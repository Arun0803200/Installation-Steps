How to create rabbitmq in windows ?
------------------------------------
step 1: go to "erlang.org" and download it for windows.

step 2: go to "https://www.rabbitmq.com/install-windows.html" and download for 32 or 64 bits.

step 3: Install the both flat-forms.

step 4: open terminal and navigate the path to "C:\Program Files\RabbitMQ Server\rabbitmq_server-3.12.0\sbin".

step 5: put "rabbitmq-plugins enable rabbitmq_management" after gie enter and acces for something.

step 6: go to chrome and search "localhost:15672"
